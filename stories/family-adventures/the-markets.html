THE MARKETS

Bluey, Bingo, Bandit, Chilli

"'Who wants to go to the markets?' Chilli asked. Bluey and Bingo both jumped up and down. 'Me! Me! Me!'"

"The markets were full of colours and smells. There were stalls with fresh fruit, flowers, and homemade cupcakes."

"Bingo found a stall selling tiny paintings. 'Would you like to paint your own?' the lady asked. Bingo's eyes went wide."

"Bluey found a man playing guitar and danced along. Bandit did his famous silly dance and other kids joined in!"

"Chilli bought strawberries and flowers. 'Every Saturday should start like this,' she said with a smile."

"Bluey used her pocket money to buy Bingo a wooden butterfly. 'You are the best big sister in the whole world!'"

"Bandit carried two tired puppies on his shoulders all the way home. 'Best markets ever,' Bluey yawned."
